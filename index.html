Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: About 45 minutes
  • Serves: 42 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 1 cup
Butter 2 sticks(ROOM TEMPERATURE!!)
Brown Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla 1 tsp
Flour 3 cups
Salt 1 tsp
Baking Soda 1 tsp

Step 1: Mix the dry ingredients and set it aside.

Step 2: Mix other ingredients and slowly mix in dry ingredients. Don't over mix.

Step 3: Add the milk chocolate chips or semi sweet chocolate chips.Also good with half white chocolate and half semi sweet.

Step 4: Scoop the dough with an icecream scoop, don't mold into balls.

Step 5: Bake at 375 for 11-12 minutes.

S'more Cookies*

  • Time: About 32 minutes
  • Serves: 2-3 dozen cookies
Ingredients Quantity
Sugar 1/3 cup
Butter, Softened 3/4 cup
Brown Sugar 1 cup
Eggs 2 large
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp
Flour 1 3/4 cups
Salt 1/2 tsp
Baking Soda 1 tsp
Graham Craker Crumbs 1 cup (about 9 rectangles)
Chocolate Chips 2 cups
Miniature Marshmallow Bits 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper. Set aside.

Step 2: Using an electric stand mixer fitted witht the paddle attachment or in a bowl with a handheld electric mixer, add the butter, brown sugar, granulated sugar, baking soda, and salt.

Step 3: Add the eggs and vanilla and mix until evenly combined.

Step 4: Add the flour and graham cracker crumbs and mix until just barely combined; a few dry streaks are ok.

Step 5: Add the chocolate chips or chunks and marshmallow bits until incorporated.

Step 6: Scoop the dough by heaping tablespoons ontothe prepared baking sheets, spacing at least 2-inches apart.

Step 7: Bake for 10-12 minutes until just golden on the edges and a little crackly on top. Let the cookies sit for a few minutes on the baking sheet before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
